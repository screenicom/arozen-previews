Permissions needed

To find and connect your diffuser, the app may need access to location (Android) and Bluetooth. We’ll only use these during setup. Exact permissions depend on your device and connectivity type.

Put diffuser in pairing mode

Make sure the diffuser is powered on. Then press and hold the pairing button until the indicator light starts blinking (see your product manual for the exact button).

If the diffuser is already paired to another app, you may need to reset it first.

Connect to Wi‑Fi

Select the same 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi network your phone uses. The diffuser does not support 5 GHz networks.

Pairing…

Keep the diffuser powered on and nearby. This may take up to a minute.

(In the prototype, use the buttons below to simulate success or failure.)

Diffuser added

Your diffuser is now connected. You can control it from the device list.

Done

Pairing failed

We couldn’t connect to the diffuser. Check that it’s in pairing mode, that you’re on the right Wi‑Fi network, and try again.
